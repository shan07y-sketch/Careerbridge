# CareerBridge User Manual - Version 1.0.0

This guide details steps for student applicants, recruiters, and university coordination placement officers.

---

## 1. Student Applicant Instructions

### Complete Onboarding Profile
1. Log in and navigate to `/student/onboarding`.
2. Input academic info, target departments, skills, and resume.
3. Once completed, your match suitability scores will update.

### Apply for Jobs
1. Navigate to `/student/jobs` to search jobs.
2. Select any listing to view company profile details, skills fit metrics, and AI recommendations.
3. Click **Apply Now** to submit your resume.

---

## 2. Recruiter & Employer Portal Guide

### Manage Job Life Cycles
1. Navigate to the job creation manager.
2. Create postings in **Draft** mode, then transition to **Published**.
3. Closed or Paused jobs can be toggled without deleting historical candidate details.

### Review Candidates
1. Review applicants ranking based on AI match suitability.
2. Schedule interviews and transition candidate status (Applied ➔ Screening ➔ Offer).

---

## 3. University Placement Officers Manual

### Student Verification Status
1. Navigate to the verification pool dashboard.
2. Transition student academic reviews from **Pending** to **Verified** to enable placement eligibility.
